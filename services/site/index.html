FAQ

Is it really free?

Completely. The app costs nothing and always will. The only paid thing is optional theme packs — a way to support development if you feel like it. Everything that makes BlammyTV work is free.

What content is provided?

None. BlammyTV is a media player — it provides no channels, streams, or content of any kind. You bring your own IPTV subscription, playlists, or Stremio/AIOStreams setup, and BlammyTV plays them beautifully.

What sources are supported?

Xtream Codes logins, M3U playlists, Stalker portals, and Stremio / AIOStreams manifests. Add as many as you like and BlammyTV brings them together in one app.

Accounts?

None, ever. There's nothing to sign up for and no password to remember. Your sources are stored locally on your own device. Even a Themes Pass needs no login — the key you get by email is the only credential, and it activates on up to 3 devices.

Themes key lost! Help!

Your key was emailed to you the moment you bought it — check your inbox (and spam) for the purchase email. Still stuck? Reach out with the email you used at checkout and we'll get you sorted.
